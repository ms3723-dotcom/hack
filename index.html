A self-guided tour of the moments that shaped this institution. These are not the stories told in brochures. These are the others.

Welcome to the Cornell University digital archives and campus orientation portal. Since our founding in 1865, Ezra Cornell’s vision of “any person, any study” has been the bedrock of our pedagogy. However, a great institution is built not only on its successes but on its silences. This tour serves to provide prospective students and alumni with a comprehensive view of the architectural and historical anomalies that define the Ithaca experience.
